Prof. Cheng published paper in IEEE Communications Surveys & Tutorials


  The paper titled “Channel Nonstationarity and Consistency for Beyond 5G and 6G: A Survey” by Xiang Cheng, Ziwei Huang, and Lu Bai presents a comprehensive survey. First, the definitions of channel non-stationarity and channel consistency from mathematical and physical perspectives are given. Moreover, the survey presents the necessities of capturing channel non-stationarity and channel consistency in various wireless communication applications. Recent advances in capturing channel non-stationarity and channel consistency are further overviewed. Simulation results concerning channel non-stationarity and channel consistency are provided and analyzed, where the difference between channel non-stationarity and channel consistency is demonstrated to facilitate insight. Finally, potential future research directions are discussed.

  IEEE Communications Surveys & Tutorials is the most authoritative review journal in the field of computer and communication. It represents the academic frontier and future development direction of the field. The latest impact factor of the journal is 33.84, ranking first among more than 100 SCI journals under IEEE.

  Citation: X. Cheng, Z. Huang, and L. Bai, “Channel nonstationarity and consistency for beyond 5G and 6G: A survey,” IEEE Commun. Surveys Tutor., vol. 24, no. 3, pp. 1634-1669, third-quarter 2022.
